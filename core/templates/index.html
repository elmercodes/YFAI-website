ONBOARDING PROCESS

Efficiency is at the heart of our mission, and we focus on this from the start.

  • FIRST DATE

    See If We Are A Good Fit

    As much as we're sure you'd love to talk to us all day, your busy. We get it. As a business owner you probably want to make the most of your time and so in the beginning, we can have a quick 15 minute call to see if your needs and our abilities align. Questions about anything related are recommended. 

  • LETS TAKE IT FORWARD

    Learn More About Your Business

    The second call is longer, usually about 30 minutes and here we learn more about your business and all of its various process. We discuss  your pain points and suggest some ideas depending on the complexity. At the end we have notes on your business and what you want, allowing us to formulate solutions for our next call.

  • ITS GETTING SERIOUS

    Explain Your Custom Solutions And Commit

    Here we explain the solution plan we created for your business and give you an idea of what your monthly expenses would be (for the usage of the existing AI tools and services). If you want to proceed to the last phase, a portion of the agreed upon payment will need to be completed.

  • PARTNERS IN SUCCESS

    Solutions Fully Implented

    We've implemented the agreed upon solutions/services and your satisfied with the result. We can edit minor changes if need be. You then complete the remainder of the agreed upon payment and we discuss next steps if any.

    Origins

    The inspiration behind YFAI

    Long is the story that AI will take away many jobs and then control the world. I say let it! The first part that is. While working on completing my Bachelors of Science in Computer Science, one of my professors was discussing an articles about AI and automation. Being ever so interested in the topic I gave it all my attention. The article discussed that AI is getting better and better and that while it may take away many jobs, it would create many more. I think being the IT nerd in the family made the idea of YFAI come to me instantly. I came to enjoy helping out my parents with tech related tasks and I approach helping out businesses in the same manner.
